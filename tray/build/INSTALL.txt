BEFORE OPENING VAULTGATE
========================

macOS may show: "VaultGate is damaged and can't be opened."

This is a Gatekeeper security warning for apps not notarized by Apple.
To fix it, paste this command into Terminal and press Return:

    xattr -r -d com.apple.quarantine /Applications/VaultGate.app

Then open VaultGate from Applications as usual.
